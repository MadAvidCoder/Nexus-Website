Who are we?

We are Nexus. A STEM Racing team comprised of five students from Trinity Grammar School, Kew. We have a passion for innovation, engineering, and teamwork.

  • United by our shared love for STEM, we aim to push the bounds of innovation, creating designs that excel on the track.
  • With 3 podium finishes under our belt, we are back once again, ready to showcase our skills and dedication, as we reach for new heights.
  • We are competing in the Victorian State Finals, in late October. We're ready to race, ready to perform, and ready to prove ourselves once more.
  • Follow us on Instagram for updates on our journey.

The Competition

What is STEM Racing?

Proudly supported by Formula 1, STEM Racing is an exciting competition, engaging students in the design, construction and racing of miniature F1 Cars.

  • STEM Racing acquaints students with many real-life skills, all the way from engineering to project management, marketing, and teamwork.
  • It gives students hands-on experience with design software, manufacturing tools, and presentation skills, all in a team-based environment.
  • Participants work to strict industry standards, creating professional portfolios, marketing strategies, and engineering innovations to compete at the highest level, striving to be crowned World Champions.
